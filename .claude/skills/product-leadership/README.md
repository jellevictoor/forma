# product-leadership

Operate as a Director or CPO leading product organizations — covering portfolio management, executive alignment, board communication, team structure design, and product operating rhythms.

## Overview

`product-leadership` is the Leadership System skill — it addresses the people, structure, and communication challenges of leading a product organization, not just building products. It covers PM hiring and career ladders, portfolio-level prioritization, board-level product narrative, OKR alignment, and operating rhythm design for product orgs at scale.

Part of the **Modern Product Operating Model collection** alongside `product-strategy`, `product-discovery`, `product-architecture`, `product-delivery`, and `ai-native-product`.

## When to Use

- Managing a product portfolio across multiple teams or product lines
- Aligning executive stakeholders on product strategy and investment priorities
- Communicating product direction to the board or investors
- Designing team structures, hiring plans, and PM career ladders
- Establishing operating rhythms (reviews, retrospectives, planning cadences) for a product org
- Building a product culture of discovery, ownership, and continuous improvement
- Onboarding as a new CPO or VP of Product and assessing the org

## What is Included

- `SKILL.md` — Core workflow: portfolio management, executive alignment, board narrative, team structure design, and operating rhythm setup
- `templates/` — Supporting templates for product reviews, team structures, and board narratives
- `evals/evals.json` — 3 realistic test cases with assertions for trigger accuracy and workflow correctness
- `evals/trigger-eval.json` — 20 queries (10 should-trigger / 10 should-not-trigger) for description optimization

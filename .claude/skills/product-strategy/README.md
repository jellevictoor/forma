# product-strategy

Build product strategy that drives real choices — defining where to play and how to win, creating strategy canvases, setting ICP/Anti-ICP, positioning products, structuring pricing, and planning GTM motions.

## Overview

`product-strategy` is the Strategy System skill — it helps product leaders make deliberate strategic choices rather than trying to serve everyone. It covers market positioning, ICP definition, competitive moat analysis, strategic narrative, pricing strategy, and GTM motion design. Strategy is upstream of everything else in the operating model.

Part of the **Modern Product Operating Model collection** alongside `product-discovery`, `product-architecture`, `product-delivery`, `product-leadership`, and `ai-native-product`.

## When to Use

- Defining where to play (market segments, geographies, customer types) and how to win (differentiation)
- Creating a strategy canvas or strategy on a page for executive alignment
- Defining your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) and Anti-ICP (who you explicitly won't serve)
- Positioning your product against alternatives in the market
- Designing pricing strategy (value-based, tiered, usage-based, freemium)
- Planning a GTM motion (PLG, sales-led, channel, partnerships)
- Structuring and communicating strategic bets for the next 12-18 months
- Setting the strategic context that feeds into product discovery and roadmapping

## What is Included

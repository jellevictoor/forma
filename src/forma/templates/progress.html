Fitness & freshness

Fitness Fatigue Form

Fitness = 42-day load · Fatigue = 7-day load · Form = Fitness − Fatigue

▸▾ How is this calculated?
Fitness (CTL)

Chronic Training Load — exponential moving average over 42 days. Rises slowly as you train consistently and falls slowly when you rest. Represents your long-term endurance base.

Fatigue (ATL)

Acute Training Load — exponential moving average over 7 days. Spikes quickly after hard training and drops fast during rest. Represents how tired you are right now.

Form (TSB)

Training Stress Balance = Fitness − Fatigue. Positive means you are fresh and ready to perform. Negative means you are fatigued — normal during a training block.

Daily effort score

Each day's effort is summed across all workouts. When heart rate is available: effort = hours × (avg HR ÷ 150)² × 100 — so an hour at 150 bpm = 100 points, an hour at 120 bpm ≈ 64 points. Without heart rate: effort = duration in minutes.
